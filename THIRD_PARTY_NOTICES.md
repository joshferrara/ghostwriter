## Humanizer

Ghostwriter's secondary cleanup pass (`references/humanizer-cleanup.md`, and the "Humanizer compatibility" section of the generated guide template) adapts patterns and structure from the Humanizer skill.

- **Project:** `blader/humanizer`
- **URL:** https://github.com/blader/humanizer
- **License:** MIT
- **Copyright:** Copyright (c) 2025 Siqi Chen

Humanizer is itself based on [Wikipedia: Signs of AI writing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Signs_of_AI_writing), maintained by WikiProject AI Cleanup.

**Role in Ghostwriter:** Humanizer is used only as a *secondary cleanup guardrail* to remove common AI-writing artifacts. It is **not** the primary voice source. The user's personal Ghostwriter guide always takes precedence; Humanizer rules are applied only where they do not conflict with that guide.
